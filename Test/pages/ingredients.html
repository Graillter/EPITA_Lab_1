Ingredients for 30 cookies

  1. 2 cups butter (460 g), melted
  2. 2 cups brown sugar (440 g), packed
  3. 2 cups granulated sugar (400 g)
  4. 4 large eggs
  5. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  6. 5 cups all-purpose flour (625 g)
  7. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  8. 4 cups chocolate chips (700 g)
